8.1.1. Variational Method¶

The idea comes from that

\[\avg{E} = \frac{\bra{\psi}\hat H\ket{\psi}}{\bracket{\psi}{\psi}} = \frac{\sum_n \left| \bracket{n}{\psi} \right|^2}{\sum_n \left| \bracket{n}{\psi} \right|^2} \geq E_n\]

if \(\ket{\psi}\) is composed only with \(\ket{i}\) (\(i\geq n\)).
